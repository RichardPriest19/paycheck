Outside IR35 — Ltd Company Model

Calculations assume you operate via a Personal Service Company (PSC) using the standard tax-efficient structure: a director salary of £12,570 (within the personal allowance, so no income tax or employee NI), with the remaining profit extracted as dividends. The company also pays employer's NI on salary above the secondary threshold (£9,100) and a typical accountancy fee of £1,500/yr. Corporation Tax uses 2025/26 rates (19% small profits, 25% main rate, marginal relief between £50k–£250k). Dividend tax is applied after the £500 dividend allowance.
